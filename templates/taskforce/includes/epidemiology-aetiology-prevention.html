Epidemiology, Aetiology & Prevention

This task force will consist of individuals who have experience with investigation of either the epidemiology or the genetic and/or environmental factors causing cleft lip and/or palate. The primary objective of this task force will be to understand the etiopathology of clefting across different races and environments and to plan future research or interventions.
