APEC 2017 Abstract

Important Dates:

  • Abstract submission opens 15th September 2016
  • Abstract submission deadline 5th January 2017
  • Decision on submitted abstract 15th February 2017
  • Scientific Schedule by 15th March, 2017

How to Submit:

  • Only Online Submission
  • The maximum word limit should not be more than 300 words with the title not exceeding 30 words.
  • The maximum word limit should not be more than 300 words with the title not exceeding 30 words. Upload abstract in Ms-Word (.doc, .docx) format only

Acceptance / Rejection of Presentation:

  • Acceptance of submission is subject to decision of the scientific committee which shall be final and binding.

Oral Presentation Guidelines

Guidelines for Delegates/ Faculty

  • 1. A delegate can present only ONE paper and co-author more than one presentation. The email id and contact details must be provided for correspondence
  • 2. The presenter should mention in bold the type of presentation; Original Research or Clinical Case Report/ Case Series.
  • 3. Acceptance of presentation is subject to decision of Scientific committee only.
  • 4. The abstract should comprise of Title, Background knowledge, Material methods, Results, Conclusion, Clinical relevance for ORIGINAL RESEARCH and brief introduction, case report, discussion, uniqueness of modality/material applied for CASE PRESENTATION.
  • 5. The abstract for original research should contain the results and conclusion and case presentation should have at least 6 months follow up wherever applicable.
  • 6.Previously published abstracts (in print or any electronic format) or those presented at another meeting are not allowed.
  • 7. The total duration of the paper should not exceed 10 minutes. The time assigned for discussion post presentation is 2 minutes. The stipulated time must be STRICTLY adhered to.

Guidelines for Students

  • 1. The maximum entries for paper presentation are restricted to 2 per college. Two students may participate in presenting ONE paper.
  • 2. The presenter should mention in bold the type of presentation; Original Research or Clinical Case Report/ Case Series.
  • 3. Acceptance of presentation is subject to decision of Scientific committee only.
  • 4. The abstract should comprise of Title, Background knowledge, Material Methods, Results, Conclusion, Clinical relevance for ORIGINAL RESEARCH and brief introduction, case report, discussion, uniqueness of modality/material applied for CASE PRESENTATION.
  • 5. The abstract for original research should contain the results and conclusion and case presentation should have at least 6 months follow up wherever applicable.
  • 6. Previously published abstracts (in print or any electronic format) or those presented at another meeting are not allowed.
  • 7. The total duration of the paper should not exceed 8 minutes. The time assigned for discussion post presentation is 2 minutes. The stipulated time must be STRICTLY adhered to.

Poster Presentation Guidelines

Guidelines for Student

  • 1. All poster presentations are categorized as original research, Case report and basic science/review.
  • 2. Each college can send 2 presentations. (Colleges having more than 4 pgs per year can send 3 presentations). 3 pgs can jointly present a Poster.
  • 3. In case of poster on original research the abstracts should include the aim of the study and an outline of the methodology, results, and conclusions.
  • 4. Please follow the website for presentation category and sub-category.

Guidelines for Faculty/ Delegates

  • 1. The poster topic should be either original research or case presentation.

Poster Preparation Guidelines

  • 1. The Poster presentation is not an e-poster.
  • 2. It has to be printed on a glossy/Matt paper.
  • 3. Posters Size (portrait): Maximum width: 90cm, Maximum Height: 180cm. Pins for mounting will be available at each board.
  • 4. It should include all the authors' names. It should have a clear structure and layout with minimum amount of text.
  • 5. Presenters will get 4 minutes time for presentation plus 1 minute for discussion.
